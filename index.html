    The National Aeronautics and Space Administration (NASA) is an independent agency of the US Federal Government responsible for the civilian space program, aeronautics and aerospace research. NASA science is focused on better understanding Earth through the Earth Observing System.

    The goal of the Mars Exploration Program is to explore Mars and to provide a continuous flow of scientific information and discovery through a carefully selected series of robotic orbiters, landers and mobile laboratories interconnected by a high-bandwidth Mars to Earth communications network.
